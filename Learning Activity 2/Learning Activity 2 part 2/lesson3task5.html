How to be Proactive

The definition of proactive means. Setting an atainable goal, coming up with steps to acheive said goal, and staying on track

5 Ways I will be More Proactive

  1. I will set specific times each night to work on class
  2. I will keep a detailed calendar of all due dates
  3. I will not wait until the last minute
  4. I will study over several days for exams
  5. I will ask questions if I do not understand
